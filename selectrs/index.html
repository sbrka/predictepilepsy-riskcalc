Risk-zone settings (LHH framework)

Risk zones come from the likelihood of being helped versus harmed, LHH = NNH × CIF × RRR. Adjust the treatment assumptions and both zone boundaries move: they are the recurrence levels at which LHH = 1.5 (low→moderate) and LHH = 3 (moderate→high). At the base case LHH = 3 falls at ~63% recurrence, close to the ILAE ≥60% diagnostic threshold (shown as a dashed reference line).
